About Us

Right Start connects students with life-changing internships.
We believe internships are vital for gaining hands-on experience, building valuable skills, and establishing professional networks.

Our platform simplifies the internship search for both students and employers. We offer a wide range of vetted internships across various industries, including technology, business, healthcare, and non-profit.

Empowering students is our mission. We provide resources and support to help them thrive in their internships and achieve their career goals. By connecting students with internships, we're building the future workforce.
